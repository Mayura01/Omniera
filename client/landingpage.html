Limitations

While Omniera delivers exceptional conversational AI, there are some limitations to consider:

  • Occasional inaccuracies in highly specialized or technical queries.
  • Limited understanding of slang or cultural nuances in less-trained languages.
  • Potential for overfitting, as seen by increasing validation loss in the later epochs.
  • Model response can vary depending on the quality and diversity of the training data.

We are actively working to improve these areas and enhance the overall user experience.
